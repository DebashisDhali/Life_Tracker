## ğŸ’¡ PRO TIPS

### For Efficient Tracking:
1. **Use "Done" button** for count tasks you complete fully
2. **Use direct input** when you do partial completion (e.g., 15 out of 30)
3. **Use +/- buttons** only for small adjustments

1. **Group related subtasks** under one habit
2. **Use clear, short names** for habits and subtasks

4. **Review weekly chart** to spot patterns

### For Money Tracking:
1. **Add transactions immediately** - don't wait
2. **Use clear person names** - "John (rent)" instead of just "John"
3. **Mark as paid promptly** to keep balance accurate
4. **Check balance regularly** on home screen

## â“ COMMON QUESTIONS

**Q: Can I edit a subtask's type (Checkbox to Count)?**
